Eligibility Criteria

To apply and enrol in the programme, you must have 16 years of formal education. You must meet one of the following criteria:

  • 4 years bachelor/undergraduate in STEM fields
  • Final year student pursuing a 4 year bachelor/undergraduate degree programme in STEM fields and due to graduate before July 2024.
  • 3 years bachelor/undergraduate in STEM fields + any PG Diploma/Master to show 16 years of formal education
  • 3-year bachelor/undergraduate in any field + PG Diploma/Masters in STEM fields to show 16 years of education
